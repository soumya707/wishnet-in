Instructions for WishTalk

Android

Please download "WishTalk" app from Google Play Store. After installing, follow the following steps:

  1. In the app, go to Settings -> Basic Setting -> Advanced settings -> General Settings -> Availability and enable "Always running".
  2. Then, go to Settings -> Basic Setting -> Advanced settings -> General Settings -> Call quality and enable "High All".

iOS

Please download "Bria Mobile" or any other softphone app from App Store. After installing, use the username and password you received through SMS.

Fixed Line Device

Use the username and password you received through SMS. For further configurations, please contact us.
